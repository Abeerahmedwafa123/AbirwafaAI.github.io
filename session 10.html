CapCut Study Guide

Click on each section to reveal the key steps and tips.

Workspace Overview

  • Timeline (bottom): Where you arrange, cut, and edit your clips.
  • Preview window (center): Shows how your video looks.
  • Media panel (left): Upload videos, images, and audio.
  • Toolbar (top): Undo, redo, zoom, export, speed, and more.

Uploading Media

  • Click Upload or drag files into the center of the screen.
  • You can upload from:
    • Your device
    • Google Drive
    • Dropbox
    • Auto-upload folders

Inserting Clips

  • Drag any video, image, or audio file from the left panel into the timeline.
  • Reorder clips by dragging them left or right along the timeline.

Cutting & Trimming

  • Click on a clip and drag its edges to trim the beginning or end.
  • Use the Split tool (scissors icon) to cut a clip into segments.

Adjusting Ratio

  • Use the ratio icon to choose the aspect ratio (e.g. 16:9, 9:16, 1:1).
  • Pick the ratio that matches your final platform (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, etc.).

Adding Text

  • Click Text and choose a style (basic text, titles, subtitles).
  • Type your message in the preview window.
  • Customize:
    • Font, size, and color
    • Position on the screen
    • Text animation (e.g. fade, slide, typewriter)

Adding Elements

  • Use the Elements tab to add icons, stickers, shapes, and emojis.
  • Drag elements onto the timeline and resize or move them in the preview window.

Working with Captions

  • Open the Captions section to add subtitles to your video.
  • Type each caption in the timeline track where it should appear.
  • Adjust the timing by dragging the edges of the caption blocks.
  • Style your captions (font, size, color, and position) for clear reading.

Video Effects

  • Click Effects to browse visual effects such as glitch, blur, motion, or cinematic looks.
  • Drag an effect onto the timeline above the clip you want to modify.
  • Adjust the duration and intensity of the effect for the best result.

Filters

  • Use Filters to change the overall color and mood of your video.
  • Apply a filter to one clip or to the entire sequence for consistency.

Adding Transitions Between Clips

  • Click the small transition icon between two clips on the timeline.
  • Choose a transition style such as:
    • Fade
    • Zoom
    • Flash
    • Swipe
  • Set the transition duration for a smoother or faster change.
